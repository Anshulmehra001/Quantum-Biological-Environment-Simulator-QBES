Understanding Quantum Mechanics in Biology

Quantum Superposition

Particles can exist in multiple states simultaneously until measured. In biology, this enables efficient energy transfer in photosynthesis.

Quantum Entanglement

Particles become correlated in ways that classical physics cannot explain. This may play a role in enzyme catalysis and neural processes.

Quantum Decoherence

Interaction with the environment causes quantum effects to fade. QBES models how biological environments affect quantum coherence.
